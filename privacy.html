Karol

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

Karol is a personal, single-user life dashboard (finance, fitness, recovery, goals, calendar and related tools). This page explains how it handles data.

Who runs this app

This is a private application operated by its owner for their own personal use. It is not a commercial service and does not have other users.

What data is processed

  • Data you enter — weights, workouts, finances, goals, calendar events, progress photos, and similar entries.
  • Connected health services — if you connect WHOOP, Fitbit, or Apple Health, the app retrieves recovery, sleep, heart-rate and related metrics to display them.

Where data is stored

Data is stored in your browser (local storage) and, if you configure it, in your own private Supabase project. It is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for advertising.

Third-party connections

When you connect a service such as WHOOP or Fitbit, you authorize it via OAuth. The app stores the resulting access/refresh tokens (in a secure, http-only cookie on the deployment) solely to fetch your own data on your behalf. You can disconnect at any time, which discards the stored token. Use of those services is also governed by their own privacy policies.

Data retention & deletion

Data persists until you delete it from within the app or clear your browser/Supabase storage. Disconnecting a health service removes its stored token.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact the app owner at the email associated with this deployment.
